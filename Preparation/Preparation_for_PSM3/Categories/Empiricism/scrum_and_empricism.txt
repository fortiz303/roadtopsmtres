Q: Scrum and empiricism, what's the relation between the two?

A: Scrum is founded on empiricism. The three pillars of Empirical Process Control are Transparency, Inspection, and Adaptation. Empiricism is knowledge comes from experience and making decisions on what is observed. Scrum is founded on empiricism as every Event is an opportunity to ask and to define the following: Where are we? How are we doing? Do we need to change course?
